Heavy Duty Pavers

  • Available in Straight & Round Chamfer
  • Available with Various Finishes
    • Matt Finish
    • Shot Blasted (Anti Skid Surface with Elegant Look Like Stone Finish)
    • Curled - Cleans, Smoothens the Surface and Creates a velvet Finish
    • Sand Finish - It provides a hard surface which is good to look at
    • Mono Layer
  • Available in 60mm, 80mm, 100mm, 120mm thickness.
  • Recommended for Pavement of Residential and Commercial complex, Roads, Industrial Compounds, Ports, Container Yards, etc.
  • Strength - M-40, M-45, M-50, M-55.
  • High abrasion resistant.
  • Low water absorption
