Super Tiles delivers different designs & various finishes of external flooring materials having physical properties like high strength, Anti-skid surface, resistant to wear & weather, easy cleaning and durable along with their aesthetic appearance.

Floor coverings are made out of natural and synthetic materials. Each offers a range of advantages and disadvantages in terms of durability, comfort, ease of maintainance, and ability to take colours which forms attractive designs.

The Floor covering can be divided into two segments depending upon their place of application - External Flooring Material & Internal Flooring Material. External Flooring Materials which are generally used outside the building structures i.e. Building Compound, Parking Places, Lawns, Pathways & Pedestrians, Public Places, Water parks, Bus Stands, Railway platforms,Roads, Airports, Ports, Container Freight stations etc.
