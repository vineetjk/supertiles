Welcome to Super Tiles & Marbles

HISTORY

Being in the construction material industry for the last 30 years we are constantly in the look out for quality flooring materials for internal as well as external application. With us, under one roof, you can have quality external flooring material and precast products of your choice at the very competitive price. With a strong team and manufacturing plants spread all across the country we have the ability to cater customers all over the country with competitive logistic cost and prompt and immediate service. Equipped with a large atomized capacity we can cater large orders in a short span of time.Another unique feature is that we at super tiles can set up mobile plants at customers site which can reduce the logistic cost for our customers Our research and development team is always in the process for development of innovative external flooring materials.

OUR MISSION

We at Super Tiles are committed to manufacture and supply highest quality products all over India, to the complete satisfaction of every customer and thus achieve leadership position in the market as a reliable Supplier of precast concrete products.

  • Focusing on Innovation to build products for future.
  • Best in class customer experience.
  • We provide products that are both functional and aesthetic.
  • High quality manufacturing facilities spread through out the country.

WHY US?

  • We have a large production capacity for each of the flooring material and are in a position to execute any big orders on schedule.
  • We are very quality conscious. We have set up a modern test laboratory in our factories with latest testing devices and each lot of tiles undergo strict quality control tests.
  • We are also very conscious of products development and always alive to customers' needs.
  • Fully automatic machines from UK & Italy to manufacture Kerbstones, Pavers & tiles in large quantities.
  • All machines are equipped with batching & mixing plants which ensures high strength & consistency in product.
  • State of the art material handling system with pallets, forklifts & crane mounted trucks.
  • Steel moulds imported only from UK & Italy which give high dimensional accuracy.
  • Testing of all incoming raw material & outgoing finished goods.
  • We own large fleet of trucks for transporting finished goods on time.
